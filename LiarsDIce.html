Game Instructions

  • You are the first player.
  • Your hand is displayed below your player name.
  • You are betting on the number of dice on the table.
  • After the initial bet, each player must either raise the bet count, or, keep the count, but raise the face.
  • You may call bluff on other players, and they may do so to you.
  • You may also call spot on if you think they are exactly correct.
  • If you don't think the opposing player is bluffing or spot on, you can pass and have the next player play.
